Project Name: UnityCare

Description: UnityCare serves as a bridge to connect the enterprise and individual who have financial difficulties or physical disabilities. Through UnityCare, enterprises may improve their corporate reputation by caring for vulnerable groups and may receive government subsidies as a support for enterprises caring for vulnerable groups. On the other hand, individuals with disabilities can find available jobs and attend skill development programs for financial and intellectual stability. Moreover, volunteers can gain knowledge on the way to deal with people with physical disabilities by participating in volunteering programs. UnityCare plays a role in fostering a culture of empathy and collective responsibility by publicly sharing relevant poverty information. This is crucial in raising awareness about the poverty issue in our country or around the world. By providing accessible information, UnityCare educates the public about the socioeconomic issues that lead to poverty such as unemployment or war, the impact of poverty on communities and ongoing efforts to reduce these challenges.

Technologies Used:

  • Laravel 10
  • MySQL

Project Name: Way To Avoid Coronavirus Infection

Description: The "Way To Avoid Coronavirus Infection" is an educational animation project designed to inform and engage audiences about the best practices for preventing the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19). Through visually appealing and easy-to-understand animated content, the project aims to convey important safety measures, hygiene practices, and health guidelines that can help reduce the risk of infection.
