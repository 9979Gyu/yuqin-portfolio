Project Description

The Nutrient Navigator project provides an engaging, hands-on learning approach by using technology to identify and classify key nutrients in foods. Students will build a conveyor belt model, integrate a micro:bit system, and use a camera to detect food items and display their main nutrient. This interactive experience allows students to apply tech skills in a real-world context, deepening their understanding of food's nutritional value and how technology can aid in everyday life.


Benefits

  • Students gain nutrition knowledge they can share with family and friends
  • Early nutrition education fosters long-term healthy habits in the community
  • The project encourages using tech in practical, everyday contexts
